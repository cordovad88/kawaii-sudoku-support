FAQ

How do I save a puzzle?

Tap the bookmark icon in the top bar during a game. You can find your saved puzzles from the main menu.

How do hints work?

You get 3 free hints per game. Tap the Hint button to reveal the correct value for a cell. If you have a cell selected, the hint fills that cell. Otherwise, it picks a random empty cell.

How do I change the theme?

Go to Settings from the main menu and choose from Sakura, Lavender Dream, Mint Garden, Peach Sunset, or Midnight.

How do I remove ads?

You can purchase the "Remove Ads" option from the Settings screen.
