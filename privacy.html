Privacy Policy

Kawaii Sudoku

Last updated: February 22, 2026

Overview

Kawaii Sudoku ("we", "our", or "the app") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information is collected and how it is used.

Information We Collect

Kawaii Sudoku does not directly collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All game data (puzzles, settings, statistics) is stored locally on your device only.

Advertising

The app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google may collect and use data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. This may include:

  • Device identifiers
  • IP address
  • Usage data and ad interaction data

For more information on how Google uses data, please visit: Google Privacy Policy

In-App Purchases

The app offers an option to remove ads via in-app purchase. These transactions are processed entirely by Apple and are subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Data Storage

All game data, including saved puzzles, settings, and statistics, is stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage mechanisms. This data is not transmitted to any server.

Children's Privacy

Kawaii Sudoku does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app does not require account creation or any personal data to use.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
