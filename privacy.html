Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

Overview

This privacy policy applies to all apps published by Suki Kawaii Apps ("we", "our", or "us"). We respect your privacy. This policy explains what information is collected and how it is used.

Information We Collect

Our apps do not directly collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All app data (including game progress, settings, and statistics) is stored locally on your device only.

Advertising

Some of our apps use Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google may collect and use data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. This may include:

  • Device identifiers
  • IP address
  • Usage data and ad interaction data

For more information on how Google uses data, please visit: Google Privacy Policy

In-App Purchases

Some of our apps offer in-app purchases (e.g., ad removal). These transactions are processed entirely by Apple and are subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage mechanisms. This data is not transmitted to any server.

Children's Privacy

Our apps do not knowingly collect personal information from children. No account creation or personal data is required to use our apps.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
